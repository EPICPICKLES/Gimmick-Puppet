🌟 Gimmick Puppet Mansion 1-Card FTK Guide

Final version of the combo walkthrough for mastering the Mansion combo!

Home Combo

🔹 Step 1:

Activate Mansion of the Dreadful Dolls. Add Gimmick Puppet Little Soldiers to your hand.

🔹 Step 2:

Normal Summon Gimmick Puppet Little Soldiers. Use its effect to send Gimmick Puppet Rouge Doll from your Deck to the Graveyard. Little Soldiers becomes Level 8.

🔹 Step 3:

Activate Gimmick Puppet Rouge Doll’s effect in the Graveyard. Add Rouge Doll to your hand. Then, activate Rouge Doll’s effect from your hand: reveal any Rank 8 monster from your Extra Deck, Special Summon Rouge Doll and Special Summon Gimmick Puppet Cattle Scream from your Deck.

🔹 Step 4:

Xyz Summon Gimmick Puppet Fantasix Machinix using Little Soldiers and Cattle Scream.

🔹 Step 5:

Detach Gimmick Puppet Cattle Scream from Fantasix Machinix to search Rank-Up-Magic Argent Chaos Force. Cattle Scream is now in the Graveyard.

🔹 Step 6:

Activate Argent Chaos Force to Rank-Up Fantasix Machinix into CXyz Gimmick Puppet Fanatix Machinix. Fanatix adds a “Puppet” Trap from Deck to hand (backup play).

🔹 Step 7:

Use Gimmick Puppet Cattle Scream’s Graveyard effect: detach Fantasix Machinix from Fanatix Machinix to Special Summon Cattle Scream from the Graveyard.

🔹 Step 8:

Xyz Summon Number 40: Gimmick Puppet of Strings using Rouge Doll and Cattle Scream. Argent Chaos Force triggers in the Graveyard and adds itself to your hand.

🔹 Step 9:

Use Strings’ effect: detach Cattle Scream from Strings to place String Counters on CXyz Gimmick Puppet Fanatix Machinix.

🔹 Step 10:

Use Fanatix Machinix’s effect: detach a material to Special Summon Gimmick Puppet Cattle Scream from the Graveyard to the opponent’s field in Defense Position. *Important: do not destroy it with Fanatix’s third effect.*

🔹 Step 11:

Use Rank-Up-Magic Argent Chaos Force to Rank-Up Strings into Number C40: Gimmick Puppet of Dark Strings. Dark Strings triggers: destroys Fanatix Machinix on your field, deals 3100 damage to the opponent, lets you draw 1 card, then Fantasix Machinix triggers in the Graveyard to revive itself in Defense Position and recover Argent Chaos Force from the Graveyard to your hand.

🔹 Step 12:

Use Mansion of the Dreadful Dolls: detach Strings from Dark Strings to summon Strings to your opponent’s field.

🔹 Step 13:

Link Summon Gimmick Puppet Chimera Doll using Dark Strings and Fantasix Machinix.

🔹 Step 14:

Use Chimera Doll’s effect: search Gimmick Puppet Terror Baby from your Deck.

🔹 Step 15:

Normal Summon Gimmick Puppet Terror Baby. Revive Gimmick Puppet Rouge Doll from your Graveyard.

🔹 Step 16:

Banish Gimmick Puppet Little Soldiers from your Graveyard to make Terror Baby Level 8.

🔹 Step 17:

Xyz Summon Number 15: Gimmick Puppet Giant Grinder using Terror Baby and Rouge Doll.

🔹 Step 18:

Use Giant Grinder’s effect: detach a material to destroy Strings on the opponent’s board and burn the opponent for 3000 damage.

🔹 Step 19:

Use Argent Chaos Force to Rank-Up Giant Grinder into Number C15: Gimmick Puppet Giant Hunter.

🔹 Step 20:

Use Giant Hunter’s effect: detach a material to destroy Cattle Scream on the opponent’s board and burn the opponent for 2000 damage.

✅ Total Damage:

3100 (Dark Strings) + 3000 (Giant Grinder) + 2000 (Giant Hunter) = 8100 damage — enough for a First Turn Kill (FTK)!
